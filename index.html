About

I'm a second-year Computer Science PhD student at Columbia, where I'm incredibly fortunate to be advised by Christos Papadimitriou and Tim Roughgarden in the Theory group. I am also an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Web Services. Previously, I graduated from Penn in 2019 with an MS in Data Science and a BS in Computer Science and Philosophy, where I worked with Aaron Roth and Michael Kearns. I've also spent a few summers working in research, software engineering, and data science roles at Two Sigma, MongoDB, and American Family Insurance.

Research

I'm broadly interested in theoretical questions related to interactions between humans and technology. In particular, I enjoy exploring problems related to algorithmic game theory, reinforcement learning, algorithmic fairness, and social networks.
